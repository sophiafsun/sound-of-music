What makes a song popular?

It's getting harder and harder for new artists to break into the music industry. In the age of endless streaming, we're exposed to more new music than ever before, at a faster pace than ever before.

That raises the question, why do we remember some songs for decades, while others never even make it into the top 100? Is there a reason why a lot of mainstream music is starting to sound the same? Imagine there was formula for chart topping songs. How would you create the next hit single?

Let's find out.
