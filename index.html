YOUR GUIDE TO CREATING
THE NEXT HIT SINGLE

What makes a song popular?

It's getting harder and harder for new
artists to break into the music industry.
In the age of endless streaming, we're
exposed to more new music than ever
before, at a faster pace than ever before.

 That raises the question, why do we remember
some songs for decades, while others never
even make it into the top 100? Is there a reason
why a lot of mainstream music is starting to
sound the same? Imagine there was formula for
chart topping songs. How would you create the
next hit single?

Let's find out.

Top 100 Songs that Hit #1

Let's take a look at the songs over the past
six decades that have reached the #1 spot
on the Billboard Top 100 chart. Songs are
sized by the number of weeks they've been
on the chart, then 100 longest have been
selected for each category.

Clearly, each of these top songs must be
doing something right.
